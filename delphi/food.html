Delphi Food Guide

Discover the authentic flavors of Greek cuisine

Popular Local Dishes in Delphi

Fasoulada

A traditional Greek bean soup made with tomatoes, carrots, and olive oil, often called the national dish of Greece.

Armyta

A hearty meat and vegetable stew, typical of the mountain regions, featuring lamb, potatoes, and herbs.

Spanakopita

A savory pie filled with spinach and feta cheese, wrapped in crispy phyllo dough.

Traditional Drinks in Delphi

Tsipouro

A strong distilled spirit made from grape pomace, often enjoyed as an aperitif with small bites.

Mountain Herbal Tea

A popular drink made from wild herbs like sage and thyme, grown in the nearby mountains of Delphi.

Local Wine

Delphi's nearby vineyards produce high-quality wines, including reds, whites, and rosés, that pair well with local cuisine.
