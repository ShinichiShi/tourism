Mykonos Food Guide

Explore the island's rich flavors, from seafood delights to sweet treats.

Popular Local Dishes

Keftedes

Fried meatballs with herbs and spices, a favorite in many Greek tavernas.

Grilled Fish

Freshly caught fish grilled to perfection, often served with a drizzle of olive oil.

Saganaki

A delicious fried cheese dish, typically served as an appetizer or snack.

Traditional Drinks

Ouzo

A traditional Greek anise-flavored aperitif, perfect for sipping on a sunny Mykonos day.

Greek Coffee

A rich, strong coffee brewed to perfection and served with a side of conversation.

Raki

A local spirit, often served after meals, with a smooth yet potent flavor.
